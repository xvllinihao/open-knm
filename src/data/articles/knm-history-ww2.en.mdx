      The Germans arrested and murdered many **Jewish people** in concentration camps. This is called the **Holocaust**.
    </p>
    <p>
      Jews had to **hide** (go into hiding). **Anne Frank** is famous for her diary.

      The **resistance** secretly fought against the Germans and helped people in hiding.
